About EduCompass

Our Story

EduTech was born from a vision to empower students in their educational journey after 10th class. In 2024, a group of students from Vignan's Institute Of Engineering For Women conducted a comprehensive survey in the STBL area of Kapujaggarajupeta, Visakhapatnam. The survey revealed a significant gap in awareness about educational pathways and a pressing need for mental health support among students transitioning from 10th class.

Driven by these findings, we embarked on a mission to create a centralized platform that addresses these crucial needs. EduTech is the result of our commitment to making a positive impact in our community.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Local Insights: Our project is rooted in real, local community needs identified through extensive research.
  • Holistic Approach: We address both informational needs and mental well-being support.
  • Expert Collaboration: We work with educational institutions, industry professionals, and mental health experts to provide accurate and relevant guidance.
  • Tech-Enabled Solutions: Our user-friendly platform makes information accessible to all.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student in our community and beyond has the knowledge, support, and confidence to make informed decisions about their educational and career paths. We strive to be the go-to resource for students and parents navigating the critical transition after 10th class.

Join Us

Whether you're a student seeking guidance, a parent looking for information, or a professional wanting to contribute your expertise, we welcome you to join our community. Together, we can build a brighter future for the next generation.
